3. Organizing Meaningful Science Learning Environments


Current theories of intelligence as a multidimensional phenemenon emphasize the many ways in which students know, understand, perceive, learn, and process information as they make sense of their world. The learner-centered environment is consistent with instructional practices that emphasize sensitivity to students' prior experiiences, beliefs, knowledge, skills, and habits of mind.
