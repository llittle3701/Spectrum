Case study 6.2: Miss Davis Sparks John's Understanding


Ms. Davis, an experienced and dedicated teacher , had previously taught a lesson on electricity to her middle school students and assigned them follow-up readings in their text book. One of her students, John, was having a particularly difficult time understanding several of the key concepts. Ms. Davis was not quite sure what to do but decided to use probing questions and analogical reasoning to help John inquire into the nature of electricity.
